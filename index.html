Administrator

This role is vital for organizations, schools, and universities that use Moodle for their e-learning and training needs, as the administrator ensures the platform runs smoothly and effectively.

Developer

Due to Moodle's open-source nature, developers often engage with the Moodle community through forums, contributing to projects, and staying up-to-date with the latest developments.

Educator

It goes beyond simply knowing how to use Moodle's features. Teaching practices use Moodle's tools to create effective and engaging learning experiences.

Quality Assurance (QA)

It reduces the long-term cost of development by catching errors early in the "Shift Left" approach, where testing is integrated as early as possible in the development cycle.
